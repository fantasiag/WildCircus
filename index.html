CIRCUS TEAM

We are mind-breakers

Established in 1974. Based in California.

Back in the days, we were family members enjoying acrobatics and magic tricks. Thanks to our perseverance and our audience growing each day, we expand to something bigger.
Today, we are one of the largest shows company. Our 95 members are proud to be a part of this journey.

Thanks to all of you whom made it possible !
